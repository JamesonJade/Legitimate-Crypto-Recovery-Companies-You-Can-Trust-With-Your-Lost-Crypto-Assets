How do I protect my cryptocurrency assets from loss?
You can protect your assets by adhering to the recommended data security practices to protect your assets. Use complex passwords, keep the wallet's firmware updated, store your seed phrase securely, and enable two-factor authentication for your crypto accounts.
What are hardware wallets?
Hardware wallets are physical devices that store crypto assets offline. They guard the cryptographic keys to your digital assets, preventing unauthorized parties from accessing them.
Hardware wallets are more secure because they arent connected to the internet. Theyre ideal for storing large amounts of tokens, and the Ledger Nano is a good example.
What is multi-signature implementation?
Multi-signature requires a transaction to have at least two signatories before being executed from a wallet. This implementation provides an extra layer of security for your crypto assets.
How do I enable 2FA on my crypto wallet?
Different wallet providers have different processes for enabling two-factor authentication. You can consult your providers support and FAQ page to learn the process.
How do I protect my wallet seed phrase?
You can protect your seed phrase by affixing the words to indestructible metal plates or stainless steel. Then, you can store the phrase in a secure, fireproof container. For further security, you can split the phrase into different parts and store them in different locations.
